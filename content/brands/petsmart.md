<h3>What PetSmart Means to Canadian Pet Owners</h3><p>PetSmart serves Canadian pet owners with comprehensive supplies, grooming services, and in-store veterinary clinics. The retailer stocks food, toys, bedding, and accessories for dogs, cats, fish, birds, reptiles, and small animals.</p><h3>Treats Rewards Program</h3><p>The free Treats Rewards program earns 8 points per dollar on most purchases, with 1,000 points converting to $2 off. Members receive birthday rewards for pets and exclusive coupons throughout the year.</p>
