<h3>About Vuori</h3>
<p>A New Perspective On Performance Apparel™ Vuori makes premium performance apparel inspired by the active Coastal California lifestyle; an integration of fitness, surf, sport, and art. We’re evolving the definition of traditional activewear. Celebrating A Decade Of Vuori Back in 2015, we set out with a simple yet powerful vision: to build a brand that reflected the values we hold dear—connection, balance, and the pursuit of an extraordinary life. We obsessed over fabrics, designed with intention, and rooted ourselves in community. It was never just about the clothes.</p>
