<h3>What Amazon.ca Means to Canadian Shoppers</h3><p>Amazon.ca handles a substantial portion of Canada's online retail activity. The platform serves shoppers in cities, suburbs, and many rural communities through a logistics network that includes fulfillment centers, sortation facilities, and delivery stations spread across multiple provinces.</p><h3>Canadian Jobs & Operations</h3><p>Amazon Canada employs more than <strong>46,000 people</strong> across the country in roles spanning warehouse operations, logistics, customer service, technology, and corporate functions. The company operates fulfillment centers in Ottawa, Calgary, Brampton, Milton, Delta, and other cities. Since 2010, Amazon reports making direct investments of over $65 billion in its Canadian operations.</p><h3>Membership & Savings</h3><p>Prime membership costs $9.99/month or $99/year. Members get free shipping on over 20 million eligible items, with same-day delivery available in select cities. Prime Day (July) and Prime Big Deal Days (October) offer exclusive member savings of 40-60% off.</p>
