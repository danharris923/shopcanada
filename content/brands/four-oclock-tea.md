Tea Crafters Since 1992 At Four O’Clock, every cup tells a story of passion and commitment between mother and daughter.Tea crafters since 1992, we are based in Saint-Bruno-de-Montarville, Quebec, where we craft, blend, and package our teas with care, producing over 1.5 million tea bags daily. Our Philosophy As a Canadian, family-owned business, we bring people together through bold, high-quality teas and herbal teas. Our mixologist travels the world in search of rare plants and spices to create authentic and flavourful blends. We invite you to discover our authentic and expertly crafted blends.At Four O’Clock, we raise our cup to the health of the planet and to those who cultivate it using fair and organic practices. We create Fairtrade and organic tea blends. We use 100% compostable teabags, recycled packaging, and ingredients that are non-GMO and naturally gluten-free.Discover a world of unique flavours, inspired by nature and crafted with expertise.
