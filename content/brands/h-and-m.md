<h3>What H&M Means to Canadian Shoppers</h3><p>H&M represents accessible fashion for Canadians who want current styles without long-term financial commitment. With 88 stores spread across provinces from British Columbia to Newfoundland, H&M occupies mall anchor spaces and standalone urban locations.</p><h3>Canadian Jobs & Operations</h3><p>H&M employs Canadians across its <strong>88 retail locations in 10 provinces</strong>. Entry-level sales advisors typically earn $32,000-$35,000 annually, department managers $45,000-$55,000, and store managers $70,000-$85,000. All employees receive a discount card valid across H&M Group brands.</p><h3>Membership & Savings</h3><p>The free H&M Membership earns one point per dollar spent, with 150 points converting to a $5 reward. Plus members (500 lifetime points) get free shipping and returns. The garment collecting program gives $5 off $30+ purchases for recycling old clothes.</p>
