<h3>What Michaels Means to Canadian Shoppers</h3><p>Michaels has become a familiar destination for Canadian crafters, DIY enthusiasts, educators, and seasonal decorators. With 134 stores spread across the country, the retailer serves as a primary source for art supplies, framing services, and project materials.</p><h3>Canadian Jobs & Operations</h3><p>Michaels employs <strong>thousands of Canadians</strong> across its 134 retail locations, distribution operations, and corporate functions. The company participates in the Makers Like Me program, recruiting candidates with disabilities in Ontario stores.</p><h3>Rewards & Savings</h3><p>The free Michaels Rewards program provides department-specific coupons and advance sale notice. Weekly coupons often feature 40% off single items. Seniors (55+) get 10% off daily; educators and military receive 15% off. Black Friday and Boxing Day savings reach up to 70%.</p>
