<h3>Amazon in Canada</h3><p>Amazon launched in Canada in 2002 and has since become the country's go-to online shopping destination. With fulfillment centres across the country, Amazon.ca offers same-day, one-day, and two-day delivery to millions of Canadians.</p><h3>Prime Benefits</h3><p>Amazon Prime members in Canada enjoy free shipping, Prime Video streaming, Prime Music, and exclusive deals during Prime Day and other sales events. The Subscribe & Save program offers discounts on recurring purchases.</p>
