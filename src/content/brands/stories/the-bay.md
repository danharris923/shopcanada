# Hudson's Bay

## Canada's Store Since 1670

Hudson's Bay Company is the oldest company in North America, with a history intertwined with Canada itself. The iconic stripes appear on blankets, bags, and Olympic apparel.

The Bay offers designer fashion, exclusive beauty brands, and home goods across flagship downtown stores. The Hudson's Bay Rewards program and credit card offer points on purchases.
