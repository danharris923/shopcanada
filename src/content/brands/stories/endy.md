# Endy

Our StoryEvery Canadian Deserves A Better Night's Sleep. It Started With a Simple Belief:In 2015, we launched Canada’s first mattress-in-a-box—redefining comfort, quality, and convenience to make great sleep accessible to all.

What began as a bold idea has grown into Canada’s leading online mattress brand, trusted by over a million sleepers from the Rocky Mountains to Rocky Harbour. At Endy, we don’t just help you fall asleep—we help you Rise to Shine.

Every Endy mattress is proudly crafted in Canada using thoughtful and responsible manufacturing practices. From the first layer of foam to the final stitch, each mattress reflects the care, precision, and craftsmanship Canadians are known for.

Made with CertiPUR-US® certified foam—free from harmful chemicals and emissions—our mattresses meet the highest standards of safety, durability, and comfort. It’s our way of ensuring you sleep soundly on a product that’s as responsibly made as it is comfortable, night after night.

2015Endy is Founded in TorontoIn January, driven by a shared vision to help every Canadian sleep better, entrepreneurs Mike Gettis and Rajen Ruparell launched Endy, making high-quality sleep more accessible from coast to coast.

Not long after, the very first Endy Mattress was delivered in March, marking the beginning of redefining and revolutionizing the way Canadians sleep. 2016Benchmark for InnovationEndy was named one of Techweek’s Top 100 Innovators, an early sign that we weren’t just delivering better sleep, but challenging industry norms and setting a new standard for what Canadians could expect from their sleep.

2017First Launch to First MillionMay marked our first product expansion with the launch of the Endy Pillow, designed to pair perfectly with our bestselling mattress. With the momentum continuing to build, November marked an extraordinary milestone: securing a $1 million investment on Dragon's Den, the show's biggest deal to date.
