# Vitaly

Established in 2011, Vitaly is an experimental accessory design studio based in Toronto. With a future-looking approach to design, Vitaly produces engineered staples and contemporary statements constructed from stainless steel - the most recycled material on the planet. Vitaly’s genderless collection is inspired by nostalgic clubwear and the industrial manufacturing process itself.
