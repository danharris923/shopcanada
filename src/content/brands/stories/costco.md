# Costco

## What Costco Means to Canadian Shoppers

Costco has become a fixture in Canadian household shopping routines, known for bulk groceries sold at prices that often undercut traditional supermarkets. Membership warehouses anchor suburban retail plazas across the country.

## Canadian Jobs & Operations

Costco employs over 30,000 Canadians across warehouse staff, management, logistics, corporate offices, and specialized departments like pharmacy, optical, and tire services. The company is known for paying above-minimum wages and offering benefits that include health coverage, retirement plans, and performance bonuses.
