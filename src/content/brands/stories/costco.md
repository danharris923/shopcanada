# Costco

<h3>The Costco Experience</h3><p>Costco opened its first Canadian warehouse in 1985. Today, with over 100 locations across the country, Costco is known for its treasure-hunt shopping experience, Kirkland Signature products, and legendary $1.50 hot dog combo.</p><h3>Membership Has Its Privileges</h3><p>Costco members enjoy exclusive access to warehouse prices, Costco Travel, auto buying programs, and the Executive Membership 2% annual reward. The company's no-questions-asked return policy builds customer loyalty.</p>
