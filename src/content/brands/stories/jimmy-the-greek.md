# Jimmy The Greek

From Greece to Canada Family Owned & Operated Est. 1985 In 1963, Jimmy Antonopoulos journeyed from Nafplio, Greece to Toronto, Canada, bringing with him the flavours of Greece! Inspired by the desire to share authentic Greek flavours with friendly and hospitable service, Jimmy The Greek first opened its doors in Toronto in 1985 and has since grown to a network of over 50 locations across Canada!

Rooted in Jimmy’s vision of bringing fine dining quality to the quick service industry, we are proud to have evolved into a third-generation family business that remains committed to serving fresh, homemade, and authentic Greek food.

Whether you dine in or take your meal to go, our menu offers a diverse array of options to satisfy the whole family, so come join us and let our family feed yours! 1985 Jimmy The Greek opens its first location First Canadian Place, our Corporate location 1986 Jimmy opens its first franchise location Woodbine Centre 1989 Jimmy opened in one of Canada's acclaimed malls Toronto Eaton Centre 2001 Jimmy opened its first location on a school campus York University 2002 Jimmy expands to Ottawa for the first time St.
