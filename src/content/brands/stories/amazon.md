# Amazon

launched in Canada in 2002 and has since become the country's go-to online shopping destination. With fulfillment centres across the country, Amazon.ca offers same-day, one-day, and two-day delivery to millions of Canadians.

Amazon Prime members in Canada enjoy free shipping, Prime Video streaming, Prime Music, and exclusive deals during Prime Day and other sales events. The Subscribe & Save program offers discounts on recurring purchases.
