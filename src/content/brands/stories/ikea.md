# IKEA

<h3>Swedish Design in Canada</h3><p>IKEA opened its first Canadian store in 1976 in Vancouver. Known for flat-pack furniture, the Swedish retailer has become synonymous with affordable, functional design and memorable shopping experiences complete with meatballs.</p><h3>Democratic Design</h3><p>IKEA's design philosophy combines form, function, quality, sustainability, and low price. The IKEA Family loyalty program offers discounts, and the As-Is section provides deals on returned and display items.</p>
