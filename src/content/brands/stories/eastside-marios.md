# Eastside Marios

Eastside Marios brings a distinctive dining experience to communities across Canada.

Famous for hearty pasta dishes, classic pizzas, fresh salads, and indulgent desserts, this family-friendly restaurant provides authentic Italian comfort food made with quality ingredients.

With a menu that reflects both classic favourites and innovative new dishes, Eastside Marios continues to attract diners looking for memorable meals. The restaurant takes pride in sourcing quality ingredients and creating dishes that keep guests coming back.

From coast to coast, Eastside Marios remains a trusted choice for Canadians looking for a satisfying meal in a comfortable setting.
