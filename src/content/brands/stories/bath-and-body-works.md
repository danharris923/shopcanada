# Bath & Body Works

## What Bath & Body Works Means to Canadian Shoppers

Bath & Body Works has become a destination for Canadians seeking fragrance products, from signature body care collections to home fragrances and candles. The retailer runs frequent promotions including semi-annual sales.

The My Bath & Body Works Rewards program offers free products after earning points, exclusive member-only promotions, and birthday gifts. Semi-annual sales in January and June offer the deepest discounts of the year.
