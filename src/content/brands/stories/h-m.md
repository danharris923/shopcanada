# H&M

<h3>Fashion Forward</h3><p>H&M opened its first Canadian store in 2004 and now operates across major cities. The Swedish retailer is known for designer collaborations, trend-driven pieces at accessible prices, and its Conscious Collection sustainable line.</p><h3>Garment Collecting</h3><p>H&M's garment collecting program encourages customers to bring unwanted clothes to stores for recycling, earning discount vouchers while reducing textile waste.</p>
