# Nike

## What Nike Means to Canadian Shoppers

Nike has been selling athletic footwear and apparel in Canada for over 50 years, serving everyone from competitive athletes to casual joggers. The swoosh appears on running shoes in Vancouver marathons, basketball courts in Toronto, and soccer fields across the prairies.

## Canadian Jobs & Operations

Nike employs Canadians across 56 retail stores, warehouse operations, corporate roles, and logistics. Fox Group operates Nike-branded stores in Canada under a licensing agreement. Job postings range from part-time retail positions ($17-18/hour) to store director roles.

## Membership & Savings

Free Nike Membership offers free shipping on orders over $50, 60-day wear test returns, and birthday discounts. Black Friday brings up to 30% off. Factory outlet stores in Vaughan Mills and Toronto Premium Outlets carry year-round discounts.
