# H&M

## What H&M Means to Canadian Shoppers

H&M represents accessible fashion for Canadians who want current styles without long-term financial commitment. With 88 stores spread across provinces from British Columbia to Newfoundland, H&M occupies mall anchor spaces and standalone urban locations.

## Canadian Jobs & Operations

H&M employs Canadians across its 88 retail locations in 10 provinces. Entry-level sales advisors typically earn $32,000-$35,000 annually, department managers $45,000-$55,000, and store managers $70,000-$85,000. All employees receive a discount card valid across H&M Group brands.

## Membership & Savings

The free H&M Membership earns one point per dollar spent, with 150 points converting to a $5 reward. Plus members (500 lifetime points) get free shipping and returns. The garment collecting program gives $5 off $30+ purchases for recycling old clothes.
