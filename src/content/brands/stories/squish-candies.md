# Squish Candies

OUR STORY Founded in Montreal in 2014, SQUISH is North America’s first artisanal candy store that specializes in over 100 flavours of awesomely delicious gourmet gummies. OUR FOUNDER Meet Sarah Segal, our sweetest leader. To say she loves candy would be a huge understatement. If Sarah’s not at the office, she’s off travelling the globe, absorbing whatever she can about what’s new in the world of yummy gummies. OUR DIFFERENCE Come taste and discover the art of true candy craftsmanship that uses real fruits, spices and herbs to remind you to explore beyond the safety of raspberry and cherry. OUR EXPERIENCE Welcome to our sweet, squishy world. We are here to help you discover and re-discover your love of candy, in flavours you’ve never even imagined. About Squish Candy OUR RAISON D’ÊTRE You don’t have to be a kid to love candy. We believe, deep down in our gummy loving hearts that everyone should eat candy. Not just any candy, but artfully crafted candy with only the highest ingredients, and flavours so deliciously unique, your taste buds will dance the night away.
