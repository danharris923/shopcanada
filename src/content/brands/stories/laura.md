# Laura

Who We Are Home to two iconic and beloved Canadian fashion brands, Laura and Melanie Lyne, Laura Canada is proud to be a family-owned and operated Canadian business. From our design studio in Montreal, Quebec, the team crafts and curates fashion collections showcased by style advisors in 140+ locations across the country and on our websites. From humble beginnings to industry leaders, Laura and Melanie Lyne collections are sought by millions of loyal customers across Canada.
