# Staples Canada

## The Working and Learning Company

Staples Canada has evolved beyond office supplies to become a destination for technology, furniture, and business services. The retailer serves students, remote workers, and businesses of all sizes.

Staples offers printing, copying, shipping, and tech services. The Staples Professional program provides dedicated support for business customers.
