# Pet Planet

Who is Pet Planet? In November 1996, a mother-daughter team began a mission to help pets live longer, happier, and healthier lives, and certainly that mission still holds true to this day. My mom and I had been contemplating the idea of becoming business owners for a long time.

Eventually after extensive research into both franchise models and independent businesses, we finally decided to take the plunge. During our early conversations about our aspirations, one of us mentioned… If we are going to work as hard as we know we are going to work – we better make sure that this is something we love.

When Reuben passed, it became clear that this was our path; for him, for us, and for every customer – to help them keep their pets with them for as long as they could. ” – Pet Planet’s Co-Founder Our story started with Reuben.

First Pet Planet location in Calgary, AB. Second Pet Planet location to open – Douglas Square. Kali was the Co-Founder's Golden Retriever who frequented the store. Today, we have over 40 locations in Canada Pet Planet is proudly across British Columbia, Alberta, and Saskatchewan with stores run by passionate owners and operators that live within the communities they serve.
