# West Elm

West Elm is an American modern furniture and home decor brand launched in 2002 as part of the Williams-Sonoma family of brands (alongside Pottery Barn and Williams-Sonoma). Created to serve a younger, design-savvy customer who wanted modern aesthetics at more accessible prices than high-end design stores, West Elm has become one of the most popular destinations for mid-century modern and contemporary home furnishing in North America.

## Modern, Sustainable Design

West Elm's design aesthetic centres on clean lines, natural materials, and a mix of mid-century modern and contemporary influences. The collection spans furniture, lighting, rugs, bedding, curtains, and decorative accessories, with a strong emphasis on sustainable sourcing. The brand was one of the first major home retailers to pursue Fair Trade Certified products, and it offers a significant selection of furniture made from responsibly sourced wood, organic cotton, and recycled materials. West Elm's Local program partners with independent artisans and makers in the communities where its stores are located.

## West Elm in Canada

West Elm operates a Canadian website at westelm.ca with CAD pricing and local shipping, along with retail stores in major Canadian cities including Toronto, Vancouver, Calgary, and Montreal. For Canadians who want modern, sustainably-minded home furnishing that looks designer without the designer markup, West Elm delivers accessible contemporary style with genuine attention to responsible production.
