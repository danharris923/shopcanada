# Dainty Rice

Mission Sustainability Certifications Values Story Career Home About Us Strong roots The Dainty DNA The Dainty brand is a great Canadian story, a heritage we all share. We remain a local business, now amongst International food giants, selecting and supplying rice to Canadians family tables for generations since 1882.

Our Mission The core of dainty Is intertwined with our STORY Dainty, the proudly Canadian rice company, has been sourcing rice from around the globe for over 140 years. Today our goal remains unchanged; we expertly choose a tasteful variety of rices to provide you with an easy to prepare and affordable meal component.

Our mission is to foster a simpler life beholding strong values, starting with cooking for loved ones and maintaining a balanced diet. Our commitment to this end is evidenced through responsible sourcing, responsible milling and processing, and responsible action within our community.

This is Dainty Robert Reford, an Irish immigrant, adventurously sourced rice in the Orient, South East Asia, Europe, and South America. “The first cargo of uncleaned rice ever imported into Canada arrived today (May 26, 1882) aboard the SS Africa, from Burma (now Myanmar), consisting of 1,700 tons.

The milling capacity of the Mount Royal Rice mills is sufficient to supply three times the wants of the Dominion. ” – The Montreal Gazette 26 May 1882. Since 2015 the company is owned by the Marbour Group, a family owned and operated company.

To this day, our customers and our pride in serving you continue to propel Dainty to innovate and reach new heights in rice products. Our commitment Sustainability At Dainty, sustainability is at the heart of everything we do.

We are dedicated to minimizing our environmental impact, optimizing energy efficiency, and reducing waste across our operations. By embracing eco-friendly packaging, and cutting-edge technologies, we are working towards a more sustainable future for all.

We actively engage with our suppliers, partners, and communities to promote sustainable practices throughout our supply chain. Together, we can create a positive impact that reaches far beyond our immediate sphere.
