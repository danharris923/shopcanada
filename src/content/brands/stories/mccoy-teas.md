# Mccoy Teas

Behind the Blend Based in Mississauga, Canada, McCoy Teas brings a passion for tea that blends timeless tradition with modern sophistication. Our teas are directly imported from Sri Lanka, known worldwide for producing some of the finest, most flavorful leaves.

Inspired by a rich global heritage, we craft each blend to reflect quality, expertise, and sustainability. Rooted in fair trade principles, we foster lasting partnerships, uplift communities, and protect the natural environment.

With every sip of McCoy Teas, you experience authenticity, excellence, and a touch of Canadian charm.
