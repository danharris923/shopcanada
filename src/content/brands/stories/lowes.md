# Lowe's Canada

## Home Improvement Leader

Lowe's acquired RONA in 2016, creating a powerful Canadian home improvement network. The combined company offers diverse store formats from urban locations to traditional warehouses.

Lowe's serves contractors through dedicated Pro desks, bulk pricing, and job-site delivery. DIY customers benefit from installation services and how-to workshops.
