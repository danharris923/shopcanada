# Home Depot

## What Home Depot Means to Canadian Shoppers

Home Depot functions as the country's largest source for renovation materials, contractor supplies, and seasonal home maintenance products. The 182-store network spans every province.

## Canadian Jobs & Operations

The company employs more than 30,000 associates across its 182 stores, distribution centres, and Toronto-based support centre. Home Depot Canada has been recognized as one of Canada's Best Diversity Employers for fifteen consecutive years as of 2024.

## Pricing & Programs

The price guarantee matches competitor pricing and beats it by 10% on identical in-stock items. Pro Xtra loyalty program offers up to 4% back in quarterly rewards, with paint purchases earning up to 20% back.
