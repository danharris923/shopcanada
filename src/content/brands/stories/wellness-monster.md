# Wellness Monster

Nutrition Forward Snacks Who We Are We are a female-founded brand born from a passion for creating healthier snacks for kids and reimagining the sugar-filled snack aisle. Committed to quality, our nutrient-dense and delicious snacks are thoughtfully crafted with superfoods to nourish our tiny monsters while honouring Mother Earth.We believe better snacks spark better habits. With clean ingredients and earth-friendly practices, we’re here to help raise healthy, happy humans—one lunchbox at a time. Our Purpose We’re on a mission to create cleaner, healthier kids’ snacks that support their health and sustain the earth.We nourish kids to be their wild, curious, messy selves—with real ingredients, no junk, and snacks that fuel every adventure. Because thriving kids are a force of nature. Our Values Snack Mindfully We encourage kids to practice gratitude for the elements that grow our food and the farmers who make it possible. This awareness helps them naturally choose nourishing foods. Honour Our Tummies Our minds and tummies are linked—like two peas in a pod! Reducing sugars while increasing fiber and protein supports both, nurturing our overall vitality and happiness! Cherish Our Soils We source from sustainable farms that respect the soil and honour the environment, believing that food grown with care for the earth simply tastes better! Uncompromising Clean Ingredients We are a company on mission to create cleaner, healthier kids food products that honour the health of our children, support their bellies and sustain our earth.
