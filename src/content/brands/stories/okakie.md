# Okakie

We exist to empower women to be confident, unshakable and free.We're a clothing brand and social enterprise - supporting various women's charities in Canada. Our Story OKAKIE is a family name originating from the Canadian Ancestral Lands of Fort McKay First Nations. Founded in 2017, we are generations of purveyors focused on the empowerment of woman in Canada.Husband and Wife team founded OKAKIE with their refreshingly family-oriented and deeply personal approach to clothing and community.Each collection is mindfully designed or sourced by trusted suppliers and manufactures. Today OKAKIE is a global brand, shipping to over 190 countries and loved around the world. OUR MISSION At OKAKIE, we are brought together through our pursuit of worldwide empowerment.We exist to empower women to be confident, unshakable and free. We're a clothing brand and social enterprise - supporting various women's charities in Canada. LAND RECOGNITION OKAKIE ACKNOWLEDGES THAT WE GATHER ON THE TRADITIONAL LANDS OF THE FIRST NATIONS, METIS AND INUIT WHOSE FOOTSTEPS HAVE MARKED THESE LANDS FOR CENTURIES.
