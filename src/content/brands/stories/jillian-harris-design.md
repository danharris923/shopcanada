# Jillian Harris Design

JHD Mission Statement To be a daily escape and place where people can come to laugh, to learn and to be the best version of themselves while building an inspired and supported community that celebrates the art of creative living.

We would like to respectfully acknowledge that we work on the traditional and unceded territory of the Okanagan syilx people. In whatever way we can, we are determined to use the platforms we have for good.

That is why we are thrilled that this is the first year we are officially a benefit corporation and therefore are sharing our benefits report, here! (opens in a new tab) Our Vision To become a globally respected and loved lifestyle brand that prioritizes giving back and feel good practices while continuing to bring together all that life has to offer in a beautiful and creative way.

Core Values Community Our mission is to not only be a daily escape where our community can come to laugh, learn and celebrate the art of creative living but to share businesses, brands, and products with our community that we stand behind and truly love.

Team We are proud to be a certified Living Wage Employer and are committed to paying all employees a living wage for our region. Here at Jillian Harris Design Inc. and the Jillian Harris Family of Brands, we are determined to build and maintain an equitable and inclusive workforce for all team members.

Social Impact We are passionate about reinvesting in our local communities and aligning with organizations that reflect our core values. Our focus is on uplifting and contributing to underrepresented and marginalized folx in addition to organizations that help provide access to basic needs and mental health initiatives.
