# Michaels

## What Michaels Means to Canadian Shoppers

Michaels has become a familiar destination for Canadian crafters, DIY enthusiasts, educators, and seasonal decorators. With 134 stores spread across the country, the retailer serves as a primary source for art supplies, framing services, and project materials.

## Canadian Jobs & Operations

Michaels employs thousands of Canadians across its 134 retail locations, distribution operations, and corporate functions. The company participates in the Makers Like Me program, recruiting candidates with disabilities in Ontario stores.

The free Michaels Rewards program provides department-specific coupons and advance sale notice. Weekly coupons often feature 40% off single items. Seniors (55+) get 10% off daily; educators and military receive 15% off. Black Friday and Boxing Day savings reach up to 70%.
