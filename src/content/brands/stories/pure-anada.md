# Pure Anada

About Us OUR PEOPLE The success of Pure Anada is due to wonderful people! Clients and retailers like you, who believe in supporting locally-made, healthy, Canadian products have been the catalyst for our amazing growth.

We are so thankful for your word-of mouth advertising, product suggestions and purchases! Our team of employees is the most valuable part of our business. Positive attitudes, hard work ethics and a commitment to our processes, brand philosophy and customer service have distinguished Pure Anada in the marketplace of natural cosmetics.

OUR BOUTIQUES Shopping online is convenient, but nothing compares to the experience of visiting one of our local boutiques! Smelling the soap, sampling the lotion and swatching makeup shades is absolutely delightful.

What began as a hobby in my kitchen, turned into our family’s full-time livelihood. It’s been an exciting adventure; one that I believe is part of a greater plan and I am truly grateful for the opportunity to continue leading and growing Pure Anada.

My professional background began in hairstyling, where I enjoyed the creativity of working in a salon and the relationships I built with clients. However, over time I began to develop sensitivities to some of the products and chemicals commonly used in the industry.

I found myself relying on prescription creams for my hands and taking daily allergy medication just to get through the day. Growing up, I was always conscious of health and wellness, thanks to my mother who taught me the value of nourishing the body with wholesome, natural foods.
