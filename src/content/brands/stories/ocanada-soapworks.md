# Ocanada Soapworks

Experience the True North Difference O’Canada Soapworks crafts exceptional personal care products that nourish the skin and connect you to the breathtaking beauty of Canada. Naturally Clean, Mindfully Green Our journey centers on well-being and a holistic approach to a better world.

O’Canada Soapworks champions the idea that what’s best for your body should also benefit the environment. Embracing a non-toxic, eco-friendly philosophy, our range includes soaps, bath, skincare, and home products, all rooted in the essence of natural ingredients like pure essential oils, plant butters, herbs, and spices.

We’re committed to locally and ethically sourced elements, extending our devotion to health and sustainability to the community. With transparency, cruelty-free practices, and a dedication to nature’s best, O’Canada Soapworks offers a naturally clean, mindfully green, and holistically vibrant lifestyle.

No Filler, No Toxins - That’s a Promise At O’Canada Soapworks, every ingredient in our products serves a distinct purpose. We wholeheartedly reject filler components. Our commitment to you is unwavering: our offerings are meticulously crafted to be free from unnecessary additives and harmful substances.

You can trust that our products meet the highest quality and safety standards, carefully excluding any undesirable or harmful ingredients. Your skin deserves the best, and we’re here to provide it. Made In Canada Non-GMO Toxin Free Sustainably Sourced Not Tested On Animals Hand crafted Craftsmanship That Sets Us Apart At our Canmore, Alberta facility, every product is carefully handcrafted in small batches with high-quality ingredients.

We prioritize quality, attention to detail, and superior craftsmanship. Our unique “superfatting” method enriches our soaps with extra oils, creams, and butters before molding, providing a creamy texture that keeps your skin moisturized.

Our soaps are a sensory delight, pleasing the eye, nose, and skin. Try them. We’re confident you’ll agree.
