# Lowe's Canada (RONA)

## What This Brand Means to Canadian Shoppers

For Canadians planning home renovations or tackling DIY projects, the stores formerly known as Lowe's Canada remain familiar fixtures. The Lowe's banner has been phased out in favor of RONA branding, with all conversions expected by 2026.

## Canadian Jobs & Operations

RONA inc. employs approximately 21,000 Canadians across its retail stores, distribution centers, and corporate headquarters in Boucherville, Quebec. The company also supports around 5,000 additional employees in stores operated by independent affiliated dealers.

## Pricing & Programs

The price match guarantee matches competitors and adds 10% off. Most merchandise can be returned within 90 days with receipt. Plants carry a one-year guarantee. VIPpro program offers exclusive discounts for contractors.
