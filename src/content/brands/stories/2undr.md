# 2Undr

The vision of 2UNDR™ was simple. Inspired by the kangaroo pouch, we set out to create a high-performance men’s athletic underwear, with the versatility of everyday use. Using the most advanced construction techniques and materials, our patented Joey Pouch® separates man’s most valuable assets away from the body to reduce unwanted skin-on-skin contact.

With more support than a standard brief and improved airflow to keep you cool, 2UNDR products provide the best fit, comfort and style a man could ever need. Designed in Vancouver, worn everywhere.
