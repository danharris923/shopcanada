# Blenz Coffee

Our CompanyBorn and brewed in Vancouver, BC since 1992Discover the story behind Blenz’s 30-year journey in crafting specialty beverages. Our PurposeBlenz is a central hub within our communities. We believe in improving the lives of people and making their day brighter–providing quality products individually prepared by hand with care, genuine interactions with our guests, and a warm comfortable environment to relax in.

Crafting a smile one cup at a timeBlenz provides care, craftsmanship, and a community that people gravitate towards and love. Our GuestsWe value our guests more than anything, and Blenz is here because of you!

From the interactions and relationships we build together to serving the coffees that make your day a little better and brighter, we want to thank you for being on this journey with us, even just by reading our content!

Our CraftmanshipOur baristas produce exceptional beverages with modern tools, educated experience, premium ingredients and the maker’s mark. Each latte is a reflection of craftsmanship; perfectly steamed milk, balanced espresso, lovingly embellished with unique latte art.

Our CommunityEach of our locally owned and operated stores is strategically placed to serve the surrounding community. Everything that we build, train, and uphold is designed to create a culture of openness, comfort, and warmth for the most welcoming experience possible.

Our StoryWe opened our first store in 1992 on Robson Street, in the heart of Downtown Vancouver. As a result of our early successes, we have created an uplifting environment that our guest love and gravitate toward.

Over the years, we have taken immense pleasure in handcrafting innovative beverages with authentic ingredients: espresso, matcha, whole-leaf teas and chocolate, also known as our hero products! They differentiate us from our competitors and made us a market leader in handcrafted beverages.
