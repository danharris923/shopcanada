# EB Games

## What EB Games Means to Canadian Gamers

EB Games serves Canadian gamers with new and pre-owned video games, consoles, and accessories. The retailer operates under the GameStop parent company and has been transitioning stores to the GameStop banner.

EB Games accepts trade-ins on games and consoles, offering store credit toward new purchases. Pre-order programs allow gamers to reserve upcoming releases and receive exclusive bonuses.
