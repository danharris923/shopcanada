# Wecook

About Us At WeCook, we’re passionate about delivering nutritious and delicious ready-to-eat meals straight to your door! Our in-house chefs craft each meal with fresh, locally sourced ingredients for optimal taste. WeCook is designed to be convenient and hassle-free, with perfectly portioned ready-to-eat meals you can tailor to your needs and preferences. Our mission Simplify your daily life with tasty meal solutions that promote a healthy and balanced lifestyle! Quality Healthy, fresh & incredibly tasty ready-to-eat meals curated by our in-house chefs. Convenience Perfectly portioned, hassle-free ready-to-eat meals delivered right to your door. Sustainability Locally sourced, fresh ingredients with a focus on minimizing food waste. Canada’s #1 ready-to-eat choice + 4 million meals delivered per year + 150,000 unique customers WeCook Meals Our story started with two ambitious entrepreneurs aiming to transform meal subscriptions with high-quality ready-to-eat meals to save time in the kitchen. The household name in Quebec, Ontario, and now in the Maritimes, WeCook curates delicious meals made from fresh and locally sourced ingredients.
