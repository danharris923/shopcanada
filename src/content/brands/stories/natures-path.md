# Natures Path

1985 Arran and Ratana Stephens open Nature's Path Organic Foods - a familly-run vegetarian food company. 1989 Nature's Path opens the first organic cereal manufacturing facility in North America. 1999 Nature's Path becomes North America's largest organic cereal manufacturer and opens the second manufacturing facility in Blaine, Washington.

2000 Nature's Path launches EnviroKidz, Optimum, hot cereals, and waffles. 2008 Nature's Path signs the Sustainable Food Trade Association's declaration of sustainability. 2011 Launch of Love Crunch granola!
