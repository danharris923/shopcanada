# Lenovo

## What Lenovo Means to Canadian Shoppers

Lenovo provides Canadian consumers and businesses with laptops, desktops, tablets, and accessories. ThinkPad laptops remain popular with business users, while IdeaPad and gaming lines serve consumers.

The MyLenovo Rewards program earns 3% back on purchases as rewards dollars. Members receive exclusive coupons and early access to sales. Education discounts are available for students and educators.
