# PetSmart

## What PetSmart Means to Canadian Pet Owners

PetSmart serves Canadian pet owners with comprehensive supplies, grooming services, and in-store veterinary clinics. The retailer stocks food, toys, bedding, and accessories for dogs, cats, fish, birds, reptiles, and small animals.

## Treats Rewards Program

The free Treats Rewards program earns 8 points per dollar on most purchases, with 1,000 points converting to $2 off. Members receive birthday rewards for pets and exclusive coupons throughout the year.
