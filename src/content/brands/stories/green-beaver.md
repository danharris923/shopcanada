# Green Beaver

ABOUT USOur mission at Green Beaver is to help people access a natural and healthier lifestyle, while contributing to a more sustainable world. We offer a wide variety of safe and effective cosmetics and personal care products that are suitable for everyone in the family and better for our planet.

We believe that every family can truly benefit from purchasing natural products that are better for the body, for the children, and for the future of our planet. Our story It all started in our kitchen!

That’s where we developed our first recipes by combining our scientific backgrounds with our desire to make a real difference in people’s lives. Our longing to offer natural products was propelled by a mission to raise awareness about the dangers of chemicals often present in conventional personal care products.

We carefully select natural, organic, and locally grown ingredients. Tell me more ! Green Beaver is a Canadian company founded in 2002 with a mission to inspire natural living in a more sustainable world.

Taking care of yourself by leading a natural and sustainable lifestyle is possible. Mission at values Inspiring a natural lifestyle for a sustainable world, one product at a time. We strive to make this lifestyle accessible to everyone.

Our ambition is to establish ourselves as the most influential Canadian personal care company in the industry. We believe that by fostering values such as innovation, passion, transparency, and teamwork, we can all live a healthier lifestyle together.
