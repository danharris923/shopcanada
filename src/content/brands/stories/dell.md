# Dell

## What Dell Means to Canadian Shoppers

Dell provides Canadian consumers and businesses with laptops, desktops, monitors, and enterprise solutions. The company offers direct sales through its Canadian website with configuration options not available at retail.

## Savings Opportunities

Dell runs regular sales events including Black Friday and back-to-school promotions. Dell Rewards members earn 3% back on purchases, and business customers can access volume pricing through Dell Technologies.
