# Dell

<h3>What Dell Means to Canadian Shoppers</h3><p>Dell provides Canadian consumers and businesses with laptops, desktops, monitors, and enterprise solutions. The company offers direct sales through its Canadian website with configuration options not available at retail.</p><h3>Savings Opportunities</h3><p>Dell runs regular sales events including Black Friday and back-to-school promotions. Dell Rewards members earn 3% back on purchases, and business customers can access volume pricing through Dell Technologies.</p>
