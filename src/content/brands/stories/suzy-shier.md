# Suzy Shier

About Suzy Shier and Le Château Proudly owned and operated in Canada, Suzy Shier & Le Château bring you the best of everyday style and occasion wear in one destination. From head to toe, discover women’s fashion for every mood, every moment, and every look — Suzy by day, Le Château by night. Our Story Since 1966, Suzy Shier has delivered the latest trends at outstanding prices, offering sizes and styles for work, weekend, and everything in between. Le Château is celebrated for occasion dressing — beautiful, well-priced dresses, shoes, and accessories for life’s special events. Today, you can shop both brands together online and in select Suzy Shier locations with dedicated Le Château shop-in-shops across Canada. Find a Store Visit one of our locations to explore new arrivals, event dresses, workwear, and accessories. Find A Store ▶ Diversity & Inclusion For over 40 years, we have championed inclusivity in Canadian fashion. We celebrate our teams’ differences and create a workplace where everyone can be their authentic self. With honesty and care, we support our customers, partners, and communities — empowering each other to shine. Women in Leadership Women represent over 90% of our workforce and the majority of our Canadian customers. They lead at every level — including 99% of store manager roles — and occupy most leadership positions at head office. We are committed to fostering growth, mentorship, and advancement for women across our organization. Community Involvement Suzy’s Inc. proudly supports the Princess Margaret Walk for Cancer, raising funds for leading cancer research and care at the Princess Margaret Cancer Centre in Toronto. Fundraising runs year-round, with national campaigns inviting customers to donate and save. Team Suzy has raised over $1,000,000 thanks to the generosity of our customers and team members.
