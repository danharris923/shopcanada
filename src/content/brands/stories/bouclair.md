# Bouclair

Locally Designed At Bouclair, we believe that home starts with a feeling. Proudly Canadian. Eh! For over 50 years, our family business has proudly helped countless Canadians decorate their homes with the latest styles at affordable prices.

Why shop at Bouclair? When you shop at Bouclair, you're supporting the people behind the products - over 1,000 employees and their families, all across Canada Where bright ideas get done. Today, Bouclair relies on the passion and dedication of hundreds of employees.

Whether online, in our stores across the country, at our Montreal warehouse or design HQ, we work hard every day to provide you with the best shopping experience and look forward to helping you refresh any space for less.

Design-driven at heart Our in-house Montreal based team of designers develops collections of stylish furnishings and home accents at low and affordable prices.
