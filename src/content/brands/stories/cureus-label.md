# Cureus Label

Curious about us?Cureus Label is a luxury lingerie and intimates brand founded by mother-daughter duo, Nataly and Pilar Cure. The brand name merges the Cure family name, with the spirit of curiosity that inspires every item in our limited-edition collections. Influenced by their experiences as two generations of Latina women who confronted the stigma around embracing their sensuality and bodies, Cureus Label is committed to empowering women with confidence and authenticity, while challenging traditional norms in intimates.Cureus offers modern, non-traditional, and most importantly comfortable intimates that have no underwire ever.
